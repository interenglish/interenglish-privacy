Frequently asked questions

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app, or restarting your device. If the problem continues, email us with a description of the issue and your device model.

My progress or statistics disappeared.

Make sure you're signed in with the same account. If the data is still missing, contact us with your account email so we can help.

How do I change the app language?

InterEnglish follows your device language. You can change it in your device Settings.

I have a suggestion for a new feature.

We'd love to hear it! Send your ideas to our support email.
